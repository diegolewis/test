Early STD Detection

This application will be the first application released to the public as a prototype. The user will be able to securely submit a picture of their concern, taken directly from a smartphone camera, to get instant results and medical information on what they should research.

Dermatology

Using an accurate image submitted by the user, MIDA Derm is able to point you in the right direction regarding your concern. After securely submitting your image and looking over your most probable diagnosis, you will be led to medical information that will help you self-diagnose the condition in question.

Emergency Medicine

Using MIDA's image recognition technology to instantly provide emergency medical solutions that will instruct you step-by-step on how to correctly stabilize your injury.
